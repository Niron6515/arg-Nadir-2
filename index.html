Behind the Hamlet

Four are called where hooves once stood,
One too small to follow wood.
The last arrives, a stranger new,
Each remembered by a single hue.

Take their order, not their names,
First letters bind their silent fates.
Six in line no more, no less.
Enter them to make progress.

That order is not correct.
